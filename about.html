SILPA IS A NEW KIND OF POST-PRODUCTION STUDIO, RUN BY CREATORS FOR CREATORS. WE USE THE MAGIC OF CRAFT TO MOVE STORYTELLING FOREVER FORWARDS. Founded in 2022, Silpa Production was created by a group of creative troublemakers who wanted to see a different kind of story on their screens and in their feeds. Silpa Production has quickly established itself as a leader in visual storytelling, specializing in high-quality post-production, color grading, and finishing house. Operating from our state-of-the-art studio in New Baneshwor, Kathmandu, we deliver bold visions, thoughtful innovation, and unmatched craftsmanship to every project we undertake. We believe in the power of storytelling to inspire change and create meaningful connections. Whether it’s through documentaries, event coverage, or creative campaigns, we transform ideas into compelling visuals that captivate audiences and spark action.
